轩辕剑
Xuanyuan Sword
轩辕剑，传说中的神兵利刃。轩辕剑又名轩辕夏禹剑，是一把圣道之剑。由众仙采首山之铜为黄帝所铸，后传与夏禹。剑身一面刻日月星辰，一面刻山川草木。剑柄一面书农耕畜养之术，一面书四海一统之策。其内蕴藏无穷之力，为斩妖除魔的神剑。
The Xuanyuan Sword, a legendary divine weapon. Also known as the Xuanyuan Xia Yu Sword, it is a sacred sword of the Dao. Forged by immortals using copper from Mount Shou for the Yellow Emperor, it was later passed to Xia Yu. One side of the blade is engraved with the sun, moon, and stars, while the other depicts mountains and rivers. The hilt bears inscriptions on farming and animal husbandry on one side, and strategies for unifying the world on the other. It contains infinite power and is a divine sword for slaying demons.
轩辕帝采首山之铜铸剑，以天文古字题铭其上。帝崩葬乔山，五百年後，山崩，室空，惟剑在焉。一旦，亦失去。
Emperor Xuanyuan used copper from Mount Shou to forge the sword, inscribing ancient astronomical characters on it. After his death, he was buried on Mount Qiao. Five hundred years later, the mountain collapsed, and the tomb was empty, leaving only the sword. Eventually, it too was lost.
